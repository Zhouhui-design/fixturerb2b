   Based on my industry experience (design, costing, sourcing), I will break down every single item: Material & finish options, estimated unit price & total cost, production lead time per category.

   I arrange sea/air/express shipping based on your timeline and budget. You’ll get one tracking summary, not ten different waybills. After delivery, if any item has a hidden defect, I’ll find a solution within 48 hours.
